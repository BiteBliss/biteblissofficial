About Us

Hello, I'm Manieylene Manile, the proud owner of Bite Bliss. I have always had a passion for creating delightful culinary experiences, and Bite Bliss is the realization of that dream.

At Bite Bliss, we believe in crafting burgers that go beyond mere meals – they are a celebration of flavors and joy. Each burger is meticulously prepared with high-quality ingredients to ensure a symphony of taste in every bite.

We don't only provide burgers; our crispy, golden fries are the ideal accompaniment, delivering the right crunch to our savory dishes. What's a lunch without the proper beverage? Bite Bliss serves a variety of refreshing beverages to compliment your eating experience, including traditional sodas and unique creations that will excite your taste buds.
